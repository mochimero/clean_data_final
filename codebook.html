Codebook

The tidy data frame contains the following variables
  • Column 1: activities in 6 levels named "standing","sitting","laying","walking","walkingdownstairs" and [6] "walkingupstairs". This column comes from the files called y_train.txt and y_test.txt The integer labels are transformed using the following convention: 1 for WALKING, 2 for WALKING_UPSTAIRS, 3 for WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, 4 for SITTING, 5 for STANDING and 6 for LAYING
  • Column 2: subject labels, integers from 1 to 30 extracted from the files subject_train.txt and subject_test.txt . The integers were kept.
  • Column 3 to 8: tBodyAccmeanX, tBodyAccmeanY, tBodyAccmeanZ, tBodyAccstdX, tBodyAccstdY and tBodyAccstdZ. These are the mean and standard deviation for the measurements of the Body acceleration in XYZ direction. Since they are kept equal to the raw data, the range of the data is -1 to 1. It is normalized.
  • Column 9 to 14: tBodyGyromeanX, tBodyGyromeanY, tBodyGyromeanZ, tBodyGyrostdX, tBodyGyrostdY and tBodyGyrostdZ. These are the mean and standard deviation for the measurements of the GYRO in XYZ direction. Since they are kept equal to the raw data, the range of the data is -1 to 1. It is normalized.
  • Column 15 to 20: tGravityAccmeanX, tGravityAccmeanY, tGravityAccmeanZ, tGravityAccstdX, tGravityAccstdY and tGravityAccstdZ. These are the mean and standard deviation for the measurements of the Body acceleration without the gravity effect, in XYZ direction. Since they are kept equal to the raw data, the range of the data is -1 to 1. It is normalized.
  • Columns bodyaccx1 (21) to bodyaccx128 (148): the full measurements 128 long vector for the acceleration of the body in g units in the X direction.
  • Columns bodyaccy1 (149) to bodyaccy128 (276): the full measurements 128 long vectorfor the acceleration of the body in g units in the Y direction.
  • Columns bodyaccz1 (277) to bodyaccz128 (404): the full measurements 128 long vector for the acceleration of the body in g units in the Z direction.
  • Columns bodygyrox1 (405) to bodygyrox128 (532): the full measurements 128 long vector for the gyro of the body in radians/second for rotations in the X direction.
  • Columns bodygyroy1 (533) to bodygyroy128(660): the full measurements 128 long vector for the gyro of the body in radians/second for rotations in the Y direction.
  • Columns bodygyroz1 (661) to bodygyroz128(788): the full measurements 128 long vector for the gyro of the body in radians/second for rotations in the Z direction.
  • Columns totalaccx1 (789) to totalaccx128(916): the full measurements 128 long vector for the acceleration (body acceleration plus gravity), in g units in the X direction
  • Columns totalaccy1 (917) to totalaccy128(1044): the full measurements 128 long vector for the acceleration (body acceleration plus gravity), in g units in the Y direction
  • Columns totalaccz1 (1045) to totalaccz128(1172): the full measurements 128 long vector for the acceleration (body acceleration plus gravity), in g units in the Z direction

Note

The data frame was NOT filtered by the mean and std as the assingment said.
